My awesome project

We are the first generation to grow up alongside Artificial Intelligence... and possibly the last generation to exist before it surpasses us.

The Next Generation is a Figma prototype of an Interactive Resource to designed for young people starting their careers. The same young people who’s careers and lives are at a vulnerable and turbulent position. The same young people who aim to inherit this world in such a chaotic time. It aims to help them navigate a new era of technology as they coexist with Artificial Intelligence and its growing capabilities and be a starting point to how they would face the future.

Visually, the website combines acid graphics, brutalist structure, neo-brutalist interface elements, and cyber-minimalist gradients (without adopting the clean minimalism typical of tech branding). The typography leans into bold, confrontational sans-serif fonts, expressing urgency and resistance.

This ultramodern, anti-polished aesthetic positions the project as a statement piece — one that taps into the spirit of youth independence, counter-culture, and activism, while still feeling intrinsically linked to digital culture and emerging technology.

Accompanied by unconventional interaction patterns, Banksy-inspired imagery, and intentionally fragmented visual rhythm, The Next Generation rejects passivity as an interactive statement piece.
