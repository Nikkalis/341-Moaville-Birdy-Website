Yello, I’m Sawan Raman, I’m studying towards a bachelor of Design, Majoring in Media and Interface design. I found my interest in design and film in high school and wanted to explore it further in the University. I’ve found a collection of interests such as photography, filming, music, keyframe animation and graphic design. And I’ve wanted to create projects that hold a high level of creative vision and quality. As of lately, I’ve been focusing on working behind the camera trying to do better; and now I’ve created a short film project named Cut Short. This short film focuses on two characters, Jay and Will; played by Ravi Vathada and Johnson Manukau, who have come across a large bag of money, which caused them to be hunted by an unnamed criminal force. While on the run, jay and Will argue over the consequences of their actions and whether it’s worth the risk to be rich. In this film I have done camerawork, directing and music.
